Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

You can enroll online through our enrollment portal, visit any of our training centers, call our admissions team at +91 98765 43210, or use our WhatsApp service. We'll guide you through the process and help you choose the right course for your needs.

Start Enrollment →

For enrollment, you'll need: Valid ID proof (Aadhar Card, Passport, or Voter ID), Address proof, Age proof (for minors, birth certificate), Passport-size photographs, and Learner's License (if you have one). We'll provide a complete checklist during enrollment.

Yes, you can reschedule lessons up to 24 hours in advance through your student dashboard, by calling our support team, or via WhatsApp. Emergency rescheduling is also available with valid reasons. We understand that flexibility is important for our students.

Access Student Dashboard →

We accept multiple payment methods: Online payments (UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit Cards), Cash payments at our centers, EMI options for course fees, and Digital wallets. We also offer flexible installment plans to make learning affordable.

Yes, we offer pickup and drop services within a 5km radius of our training centers for an additional fee. This service is particularly popular with our younger students and working professionals. Contact us to check availability in your area.

We offer a 7-day full refund policy if you're not satisfied with our services. After that, refunds are calculated based on lessons completed. Medical emergencies and relocations are handled with special consideration. Please refer to our terms and conditions for complete details.
